Our Expertise Lies Deep

RDILLS-PIPE’s expertise lies deep in covering piping and electronic leak detection systems, yet our company was made up of many great businesses from around the world – all of which have contributed to the knowledge and capability that got RDILLS-PIPE to where we are today.

2020

RDILLS-PIPE Egypt receives ISO 9001 certification.

2019

RDILLS-PIPE India Pvt. Ltd. receives ISO 14001 certification.

2019

The Nasdaq MarketSite Tower, congratulated RDILLS-PIPE International Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ:PPIH) on its 30th listing and founding anniversary.

2019

RDILLS-PIPE establishes a new production facility in
Beni Suef, Egypt.

2018

RDILLS-PIPE was awarded a major contract for the largest medical facility in the twin cities, Jeddah and Riyadh, for the special forces of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

2017

MFRI Board of Directors approves to rename and rebrand the Company under the RDILLS-PIPE name.​

2017

MFRI Announces Name Change to RDILLS-PIPE International Holdings, Inc. Ticker Symbol to Become “PPIH”​.

2016

MFRI names David J. Mansfield as President and Chief Executive Officer.​

2016

RDILLS-PIPE acquires all shares of Bayou RDILLS-PIPE Canada Ltd and renames company RDILLS-PIPE Canada Ltd.​

2013

RDILLS-PIPE Saudi Arabia, LLC receives ISO 14001 certification.​

2012

RDILLS-PIPE Saudi Arabia has grand opening in Dammam in April​.

2012

RDILLS-PIPE Lebanon Tennessee facility receives ISO 9001 certification.​

2011

RDILLSAlert receives ISO 9001 and ATEX certifications​.

2010

RDILLS-PIPE Middle East (FZC) receives ISO 9001 certification.​

2009

RDILLS-PIPE invests in Canada through Bayou RDILLS-PIPE Canada Ltd.​

2009

RDILLS-PIPE is awarded the order to supply GSPU insulated offshore pipeline for the Shell Perdido Project.

2009

RDILLS-PIPE was awarded to supply pre-insulated piping systems for the district cooling and heating at the Princess Noura University in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. This project was the first and largest university for women in the Middle East.

2008

RDILLS-PIPE New Iberia facility receives ISO 9001 certification.

2008

RDILLS-PIPE sets up a manufacturing facility in Gujarat, India to insulate and jacket a 610 km heavy crude oil pipeline that runs from Barmer to Salaya in India. This pipeline is the longest pre-insulated, skin effect heat traced pipeline in the world.

2006

RDILLS-PIPE was awarded the 34 km Qatar Gas II Sulfur Pipeline Project for the first heat traced molten sulphur pipeline in the Middle East.

2006

Received EN 253 Test certificate.

2005

RDILLS-PIPE Middle East (FZC) registered in Fujairah, United Arab Emirates, as the first plant in the Middle East to serve Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries and supply to the district cooling and oil & gas industries.

2003

RDILLS-PIPE was awarded a contract for the Al Dhafra Air Force Base in the United Arab Emirates. This contract was the first jet fuel double containment pipeline in the Middle East for a military airbase.

 

1997

RDILLS-PIPE sets up a manufacturing facility in New Iberia LA, USA to serve the offshore Oil & Gas industries.

1994

RDILLS-PIPE became a public company, a subsidiary of MFRI, Inc. in January. The stock is traded on the NASDAQ National Market under the symbol “MFRI”.

 

RDILLS-PIPE acquired certain assets* of its major competitor Ricwil Piping Systems. Ricwil was formed in 1909. *RDILLS-PIPE is not a successor in interest to Ricwil Inc., Ricwil Limited Partnership, or Ricwil Piping Systems a division of Intergy, Inc.

1989

Midwesco Inc. subsidiary Midwesco Filter Resources Inc. became a public company with stock traded on NASDAQ and in 1994 merged with RDILLS-PIPE.

1983

Received Federal Agency approval for condensate return POLY-THERM with an FRP service pipe, testing temperatures to 300° F (149° C).

1982

RDILLSAlert leak detection introduced.

1980

The first major manufacturer to receive U.S. Military Federal Agency Letter of Acceptability for Class A systems.

1979

RDILLS-PIPE was awarded its very first contract in the Middle East for the King Khalid Military City in Saudi Arabia.

1969

Opened manufacturing facilities in Lebanon, Tennessee. Our centrally located facility conveniently services the entire United States and all U.S. ports of call.

 

1961

RDILLS-PIPE, Inc. was incorporated in as a division of Midwesco, Inc. a private company in Chicago, IL.

1931

Midwesco, Inc. is formed.

RDILLS-PIPE International Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ:PPIH) is a global engineered pipe services company offering core competencies in anti-corrosion coatings, insulation solutions, containment systems, leak detection systems, engineering support, field service, and custom fabrication.
