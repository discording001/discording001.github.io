Pre-Insulated Pipe System

XTRU-THERM Premier Engineered Series

XTRU-THERM Pro Series

Product Video
Series Value
Product Brochure

XTRU-THERM® is the Pro Series pre-insulated piping system consisting of efficient polyurethane (PUR) insulation, a waterproof high-density polyethylene (HDPE) outer jacket, and a carrier pipe to meet minimum project requirements.

The outer jacket’s standard color is black.  Also, available in any color, please ask RDILLS-PIPE for more information.

 

Formerly XTRU-THERM GOLD, XTRU-THERM PLUS, ULTRAFOAM FBE, ULTRAFOAM IIISE, TERRA-GARD
